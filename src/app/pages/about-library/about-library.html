N. J. Yasaswy Memorial Library

Knowledge Resource Centre of IFHE Hyderabad

About the Library

The N. J. Yasaswy Memorial Library serves as the central learning and knowledge resource centre of The ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education (IFHE), Hyderabad.

The library supports the academic, research, and professional development needs of students, faculty members, researchers, and scholars by providing access to a wide range of printed, digital, and electronic resources.

The library houses an extensive collection of books, journals, magazines, newspapers, theses, institutional publications, online databases, and digital learning resources across Management, Engineering, Law, Architecture, Social Sciences, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Finance, Economics, and other interdisciplinary domains.

About IFHE Hyderabad

The ICFAI Foundation for Higher Education (IFHE), Hyderabad, is a Deemed-to-be University established under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956.

IFHE is accredited by NAAC with an A++ Grade and is recognized for its student-centric learning approach, industry-oriented curriculum, and strong emphasis on research and innovation.

The University comprises multiple schools including ICFAI Business School (IBS), ICFAI Tech School, ICFAI Law School, ICFAI School of Architecture, and ICFAI School of Social Sciences.

Library Mission

To support teaching, learning, research, and innovation by providing timely access to high-quality information resources, advanced digital services, and a conducive academic environment.

Library Vision

To be a world-class academic knowledge centre that empowers learners and researchers through excellence in information services, technology-enabled access, and lifelong learning.

Named in Honour of N. J. Yasaswy

The library is named after Shri N. J. Yasaswy, founder of the ICFAI Group, who was widely recognized for his contributions to higher education, finance, professional learning, and academic excellence in India.
