About the Library

The University has a Central library and 4 departmental libraries attached to the Faculty of Science & Technology (FST), Faculty of Law (FOL), School of Architecture (ISArch), and Faculty of Social Sciences (FOSS). The Central library has been named as Sri N J Yasaswy Memorial Library, to commemorate the memory of Sri N J Yasaswy who is the founder of ICFAI group of institutions. The carpet area of the library is 45000 sq.ft having a seating capacity of 750 spread over ground plus two floors, centrally air conditioned, and equipped with fire alarms and CC TVs security systems. Further, availability of e–resources and completely Wi–Fi enabled campus facilitate collaborative and interactive learning, thus making the entire campus a virtual library.

All the library activities are automated using Libsuite, the integrated library management software. An online public access catalogue is maintained to help the users. All the documents are bar-coded and bar-coded identity cards are issued to the users for borrowing the documents.

The library is equipped with rich and diverse collection of national / international text books, journals, magazines, e-journals, rare books, databases, theses and so on. As on date, the library has a collection of 1,05,761 print books and over 29,701 eBooks covering various subjects like Management, Economics, Engineering and Technology, Law, Architecture, Psychology and Fiction etc. The library also subscribes to several online databases such as Ebsco, Emerald, Proquest, Prime database, CMIE, Science Direct, Scopus, Web of Science, IEEE, Springer Engineering Journals, SCC Online and Manupatra etc. The users access these databases through the campus intranet. Off-campus access is also provided through the Knimbus software tool; enabling connectivity to IFHE from anywhere around the world.

IFHE is also registered with e-Shodh Sindhu and Shodh Ganga and National Digital Library of India. The research related documents such as monographs, rare books, bound volumes, annual reports, dictionaries & encyclopedias, dissertations and theses are housed in the library reference section for the use of research. Information relating to the latest books and revised editions is gathered from time to time and communicated to all the stake-holders for their information as well as usage
