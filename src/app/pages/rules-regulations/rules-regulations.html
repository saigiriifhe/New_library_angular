Library Rules & Regulations

N. J. Yasaswy Memorial Library

Membership

  • Library membership is open to all registered students, faculty members and staff of IFHE.
  • Library ID Card must be produced whenever required.
  • Users are responsible for all materials borrowed on their account.

Borrowing Rules

  • Books should be borrowed only through the circulation counter.
  • Reference books, journals, magazines and newspapers are for library use only.
  • Borrowed books must be returned on or before the due date.
  • Renewal is subject to demand and reservation status.

Loss of Books

  • Loss of a book must be reported immediately to the library.
  • The borrower must replace the lost item with the latest edition or pay the prescribed replacement charges.
  • Damaged books may attract repair or replacement charges.

Reading Hall Discipline

  • Silence must be maintained inside the library.
  • Mobile phones should be kept in silent mode.
  • Group discussions are not permitted in reading areas.
  • Users should maintain a peaceful academic environment.

Digital Resource Usage

  • E-resources are provided strictly for academic and research purposes.
  • Downloading entire journals or databases is prohibited.
  • Users must comply with database licensing agreements.
  • Unauthorized sharing of login credentials is not permitted.

General Rules

  • Personal belongings should be kept at designated places if required.
  • Food and beverages are not allowed inside the library.
  • Users should handle library materials with care.
  • The library reserves the right to suspend privileges for misuse of resources.
The Library Administration reserves the right to modify rules and regulations whenever necessary.
